About Us

Hi! We are the creators of the Butter web app you are visiting right now. We wanted to create an app that could be useful to everyone.

As you may already know, budgeting is the bread and butter (get it?) of personal finance and financial stability. To make this easier, we've created a website that will do all of the calculations for you. All you need to do is enter your transactions, sit back, and determine if your current spending is working for you. You can also make use of our spending analysis feature to get a quick picture of what your expenses look like.

Happy budgeting!
